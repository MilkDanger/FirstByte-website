About firstByte UofA

firstByte grew out of Hack Arizona's need to provide an adequate support network for their attendees.

Since our inception, we've grown into a student led organization that constantly provides free hands on technology workshops.

We host and teach free workshops at UofA's main campus, tucson schools, and public libraries.

We truly believe that everyone should have access to a quality, relevant, and free technical education.
