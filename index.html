Who We Are

FirstByte is an organization founded in 2017 dedicated to providing free technology education. We are volunteers from the University of Arizona who believe anyone can be a programmer with the right guidance. Let us help you take your first byte of coding.

In Your Classroom

We visit high schools and middle schools in the Tucson area to introduce students to programming and show them the many possibilities it unlocks.
