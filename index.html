About firstByte UofA

firstByte grew out of Hack Arizona's need to provide an adequate support network for students pursuing new and often difficult ideas during their awesome event that takes place every January at UofA's Science and Engineering Library.

We truly believe that everyone should have access to a quality, relevant, and free technical education.

Since our inception, we've grown to a student led organization that provides free hands on technology workshops all year round.
